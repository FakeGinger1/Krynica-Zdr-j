Tree-top Tower

Walk this 1030m pathway among the tall pine tree forest of Slotwiny. This wooden lookout tower reaches 49.5 metres high which equals to 15 stories tall! The wooden path is decorated by information boards providing context about the surrounding wildlife, nature and history. Finish this spectacular walk off with a quick descent on a 60 metre slide, not just for kids! During the summer concerts are held below the tower, pairing music with an exceptional view. In the winter take the short walk to the neighbouring ski resort to make the most of the winter snowfall.
